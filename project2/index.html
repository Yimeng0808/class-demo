Music

Posters

In this assignment you will be asked to design and animate a poster that incorporates sound. This can be music, voice-over, noise, etc.

Express the content primarily with type and audio. Push the typographicrelationships including scale, quantity, juxtaposition, etc. Consider the potential of form, counterform, and if appropriate: overlap, fragmentation, repetition, depth, color, and transparency. Exploit the poster form as a stage for performance. Be conscious of how the type is anchored/oriented to the surface of the poster and activate it with sound. Can be real time or interactive.

In art, "content" is ...the pretext, the goal, the lure which engages consciousness in essentially formal pro- cesses of transformation. Susan Sontag
